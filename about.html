  • Our Story

    The idea for Vonza was born because our co-founders who are creators and entrepreneurs, were frustrated over their inability to find an all-in-one platform that could do what they wanted to do in their business.

    They were using over 20 different apps and software to run their business and exasperated that they were spending more time, money and brain power dealing with apps, software, developers, programmers and tech people rather than doing what they truly loved which is creating products and courses that are changing people’s lives.

    One day at 3AM, our CEO prayed to God for a name for this platform and he clearly heard the name VONZA. It was a name he never heard before. The domain was purchased as a premium domain and on that day Vonza was born.
